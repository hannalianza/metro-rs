Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

Everything you need to know about our products, services, and policies.

Do you offer free delivery on restaurant equipment?

Yes! Metro Restaurant Supply offers free local delivery within the greater Tacoma and Seattle metro area on orders over $500. For orders outside our delivery zone, we provide competitive flat-rate shipping options. Contact us for a custom delivery quote on large or heavy equipment.

What brands of commercial kitchen equipment do you carry?

We carry a wide selection of trusted commercial brands including True, Turbo Air, Atosa, Beverage-Air, Vulcan, Garland, and many more. Whether you need refrigeration, cooking equipment, or prep tables, we stock top-rated brands at competitive prices.

Do you provide installation for restaurant equipment?

Absolutely. We offer professional installation services for all major equipment purchases. Our experienced technicians ensure your equipment is set up correctly, meets local codes, and is ready to use on day one. Ask about bundled delivery and installation pricing.

What is your warranty policy?

All new equipment comes with the manufacturer's standard warranty, which typically ranges from 1 to 3 years depending on the product. We also offer extended warranty options for added peace of mind. Our team is happy to walk you through coverage details before you buy.

Can I visit your showroom before purchasing?

Of course! Our Fife, WA showroom is open Monday through Saturday. Come see our equipment in person, ask questions, and get expert advice from our team. Walk-ins are welcome, or you can schedule an appointment for a personalized consultation.

Do you offer financing for commercial equipment?

Yes, we partner with trusted lending providers to offer flexible financing options for qualifying businesses. Whether you're outfitting a new restaurant or upgrading your kitchen, we can help you find a payment plan that fits your budget.
